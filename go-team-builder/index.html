How to Use our Pokemon Go Team Builder

wellcome to Pokemon Go Team Builder guide. This tool helps you create and manage your Pokemon team for battles. Follow the steps below to make the most of this calculator.

Step 1: Choose Your Pokemon

In this step, you'll be selecting the Pokemon for your team. Follow the instructions below:

  1. Click on the dropdown menu labeled "Select Pokemon" to open the list of available Pokemon.
  2. Scroll through the list and click on the Pokemon you want to add to your team. You can select multiple Pokemon by holding down the Ctrl (or Command) key while clicking on each Pokemon.
  3. Once you've selected a Pokemon, it will appear as a card in the "Your Team" section below the dropdown menu.
  4. If you want to remove a Pokemon from your team, click the "Remove" button on its card.

Step 2: View Your Team

Once you've selected your Pokemon team, you can view it in the "Your Team" section. The section will display cards for each Pokemon in your team, including the Pokemon's image, name, and other details.

Step 3: Combat Power (CSA)

In the Combat Power (CSA) section, you can see the details of the Pokemon in your team. Each Pokemon card displays the Pokemon's image, name, type, height, weight, base experience, abilities, and stats.

Step 4: Analyze Your Team

As you select and modify your team, the Combat Power (CSA) section will automatically update to reflect the Pokemon in your team. This information can help you analyze your team's strengths and weaknesses.

Step 5: Optimize Your Team

Use the information from the Combat Power (CSA) section to optimize your team. Consider the Pokemon's types, abilities, and stats to create a well-rounded and powerful team for battles.

That's it! You now know how to use the Pokemon Go Team Builder calculator. Enjoy creating and optimizing your Pokemon team for battles!

Note: This guide assumes that you have a basic understanding of Pokemon and Pokemon Go. It's recommended to have knowledge of Pokemon types, abilities, and stats for effective team building.
