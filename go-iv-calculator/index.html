Pokemon Go IV Calculator

Welcome to our Pokémon Go IV Calculator! This user-friendly tool helps trainers like you determine the Individual Values (IVs) of your Pokémon. By inputting your Pokémon's CP, HP, and Stardust cost, our calculator will calculate the Attack IV, Defense IV, Stamina IV, and overall Total IV percentage.

Accurate IV knowledge is crucial for making informed decisions, such as powering up the right Pokémon and forming a strong battle team.

Our goal is to provide you with an easy-to-use solution that enhances your Pokémon Go gameplay. Best of luck on your journey to unlocking your Pokémon's full potential!

How to Use Pokémon Go IV Calculator

Welcome to our Pokémon Go IV Calculator! This guide will walk you through the steps of using our calculator to determine the Individual Values (IVs) of your Pokémon.

Step 1: Select a Pokémon

Enter the name of the Pokémon you want to calculate IVs for in the "Search Pokemon" input field. As you start typing, the calculator will suggest Pokémon names to choose from. Select the desired Pokémon from the list.

Step 2: Enter Pokémon Details

Provide the following information:

  • CP: Enter the Combat Power (CP) of your Pokémon. This value represents the Pokémon's overall strength.
  • HP: Enter the Hit Points (HP) of your Pokémon. HP indicates the amount of damage your Pokémon can sustain.

Step 3: Select Stardust Amount

Select the appropriate amount of Stardust from the dropdown menu. The available options will vary based on the selected Pokémon. Stardust is used to power up and level up your Pokémon.

Step 4: Calculate IVs

Click the "Calculate IV" button to calculate the IV values based on the provided information. The calculator will process the data and determine the IV values for Attack, Defense, and Stamina.

Step 5: Review Results

The calculator will display the calculated IV values for Attack, Defense, and Stamina, along with the overall Total IV percentage. This percentage represents the overall potential of your Pokémon, with higher percentages indicating better IV values.

Step 6: Interpret the IV Values

Interpret the IV values to understand your Pokémon's potential:

  • Higher IV values indicate better potential for Attack, Defense, and Stamina.
  • Take note of the individual IV values for each stat to identify your Pokémon's strengths and weaknesses.

Follow these steps to make the most out of our Pokémon Go IV Calculator. Enjoy optimizing your Pokémon and enhancing your gameplay experience!
